Daily wellness tips
  • Follow a predictable daily routine with a visual schedule
  • Use sensory tools — fidget toys, weighted blanket, noise-cancelling headphones
  • Take movement breaks between tasks
  • Practice naming feelings using pictures or emoji charts
  • Get enough sleep and time outdoors every day
Evidence-based supports (professional guidance needed)
  • ADHD Behavioral parent training, school accommodations (IEP/504)
  • Autism + Anxiety Adapted CBT with visual supports
  • PTSD / Trauma Trauma-Focused CBT (TF-CBT)
  • Generalized Anxiety Play therapy or CBT
Medications: Only use medicines prescribed by a doctor (e.g. stimulants for ADHD, SSRIs for anxiety in some cases). Never start or stop without medical advice.
